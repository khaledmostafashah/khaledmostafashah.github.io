The Shah Foundation is basically a social welfare organization named after Hazrat Shah Shamsuddin Bukhari (May Allah have Mercy on him), who among many brought the light of Islam to Bangladesh. The saint settled in the village of Kurikhai, PS. Katiadi in the District of Kishoreganj.

The foundation is the social welfare organization run by the descendents of late saint. Apart from other broad ranging activities Shah Foundation is also a research body. Here in this Website the writings of two members of the family are now provided.
